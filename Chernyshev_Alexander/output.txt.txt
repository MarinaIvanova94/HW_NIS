Free Wi-Fi and karaoke are featured at Reavil Hotel, located 5 minutes’ drive from Arzamas Train Station. The hotel offers air-conditioned rooms with a satellite flat-screen TV.
All the classic-style rooms have a minibar and a safety deposit box. Bathrooms come with a hairdryer.
The Reavil’s restaurant serves European and Russian cuisine, as well as a continental breakfast. Guests can enjoy their meal and sing their favourite songs in the karaoke room of the restaurant.
Set in the historical centre, the hotel provides good access to Arzamas’s main attractions, such as St. Nicholas’s Nunnery and the Resurrection Cathedral 5 minutes’ walk away.
Sobornaya Ploshchad bus stop is 650 feet from Reavil Hotel, offering links to the city districts. A shuttle to Strigino Airport (68.4 miles) is available.
